Tact

Feeling life's ups and downs? The Quran's wisdom is here to help! Each surah offers comfort, guidance, and strength.
  • Sad or stressed? Surah Ad-Duha (93) and Surah Al-Inshirah (94) remind us that ease follows hardship.
  • Feeling grateful? Surah Ar-Rahman (55) celebrates the countless blessings from Allah.
  • Need guidance? Surah Al-Fatihah (1) is your go-to for clarity and direction.
  • Seeking forgiveness? Surah Az-Zumar (39:53) reassures with Allah’s mercy and forgiveness.
  • Wanting comfort? The story in Surah Yusuf (12) teaches patience and resilience.
