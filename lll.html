Islam

Islam is a comprehensive and universal religion that includes beliefs, worships,ethics, and social laws. Below, the principles and laws of Islam are briefly and systematically explained from the basics to the advanced aspects.

Basic Principles of Islam

Basic Principles of Islam Islam is based on five principles (pillars of the religion).

  1. Shahada (Declaration of Faith)
  2. Prayer (Salah)
  3. Fasting (Sawm)
  4. Zakat (Obligatory Charity)
  5. Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Basic Beliefs of Islam

  1. Tawhid
  2. Justice
  3. Prophethood
  4. Imamate
  5. Afterlife

Morality in Islam

Islam places special emphasis on adhering to morality

  • Honesty and Truthfulness: A Muslim must be honest
  • Kindness: A Muslim should be kind to humans and animals
  • Respecting Others' Rights: Including respect for parents, spouse, neighbors, and the community.
  • Humility: Avoiding arrogance.
  • Avoidance of Sin: Including lying, backbiting, bribery, adultery, and theft.

Ritual and Personal Laws

  • Purity and Cleanliness
  • Dress and Hijab
  • Permissible Foods and Drinks
  • Islamic Slaughter

Criminal Laws in Islam

  • Retribution (Qisas): Punishment for intentional murder, which can be forgiven with the victim’s family’s consent.
  • Hudud: Fixed punishments for specific crimes, such as flogging for adultery or theft (under certain conditions).
  • Discretionary Punishments (Ta'zir): Penalties left to the discretion of the Islamic ruler.

Customs and Traditions in Islam

Prayers and Supplications: Reciting various prayers throughout the day, including morning and evening supplications.
Islamic Holidays:

  • Eid al-Fitr: End of Ramadan.
  • Eid al-Adha: During Hajj.
  • Islamic Burial: Washing and shrouding the deceased according to Islamic laws.

Other Important Laws and Notes

  • Respect for Nature and Animals: Cutting down trees unnecessarily and harming animals are prohibited.
  • Rights of Non-Muslims: Islam emphasizes peaceful coexistence with followers of other religions.
  • Jihad: Defense of the country and religion against enemies, but with specific principles.

This is a summary of the laws and teachings of Islam.
